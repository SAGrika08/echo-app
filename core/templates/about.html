About Echo

Echo is a personal ambient sound mixer. Record sounds from your microphone, upload audio files, or link sounds from the web, then layer them together, balance the volumes, and save your mix as a scene. Come back to it any time.

The right atmosphere changes everything. The way rain against a window makes writing easier. The way a fireplace makes 2am feel safe. Echo is built around that idea, sound is not background noise, it is the shape of a moment. Your moments deserve to be designed.

How it works

Add Record, upload, or link any sound into your personal library.
Mix Open the Mixer, toggle sounds on, set each volume independently, and preview the layered atmosphere in real time.
Save Name your scene, set it to private or public, and save it. Echo keeps it for you. Revisit, refine, or start over.
